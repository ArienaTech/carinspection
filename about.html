About

Welcome To
CAR INSPECTION & SERVICES

Award Car Inspection Services based in Auckland was formed in xxxx by an enterprising motor engineer.

This service soon caught on and as the business developed, the reputation of Car Inspection Services became well established. As the years have passed, Award Car Inspection has had to adapt in response to the advances in technology and the resulting development in motor vehicles.

Over the last decade in particular, with more complex and broadening technologies, the introduction of hybrid and electric vehicles and the emergence of hydrogen power, we are experiencing the most significant developments in the history of the motor vehicle. It is essential that testing and reporting technologies be continuously in step to provide our clients with the best levels of information possible.

Celebrating our 51st year, Car Inspection Services, as in 1967, continues to aim to provide the most comprehensive mobile vehicle inspection service available in Auckland and Wellington.

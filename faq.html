FAQ

Q. How long does an inspection take?

As each inspection is tailored to the specific model vehicle and the particular testing requirements necessary, the duration of the inspection time will vary accordingly. The typical allowable time for a simple vehicle with easy access would be 1-1.5 hours.

Other vehicles with greater levels of complexity may mean an allowable time of 2-2.5 hours and there are others again that can require up to 3-4 hours. We can advise the time needed at the time of booking.
